## The Project

Hunter's Moon is a roguelike deck builder set in the Sovereign Syndicate universe, praised for its immersive atmosphere and strong voice acting. As a spin-off adventure, it expands the world while introducing new gameplay mechanics.

**[View on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3877790/Hunters_Moon_A_Sovereign_Syndicate_Adventure/)**

## My Role

As the existing **Narrative Director** at Crimson Herring, I was introduced to the project by our Game Producer and Creative Director. With a foundation in the Syndicate universe, I worked to bring the game to launch.

### Design Responsibilities

- Worked with our Creative Director to bring the storyline to fruition
- Met with the Game Director to plan narrative work
- Planned quests, missions, and interactions

### Writing Responsibilities

- Wrote dialogue, scene descriptions, and interactions
- Reviewed and revised deliveries from junior writers
- Tested dialogue and gameplay within Unity
- QA and bug testing for the game overall
